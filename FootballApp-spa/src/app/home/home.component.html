Features

At this very first version of our platform, users will be given a set of options, presented below.

Personal information.

Provide all your personal information that will help people identify you.

  • Fill in your First and Last Name.
  • Provide information about the teams you have played, the position and city..
  • Make your social media links available.

Teams information

As a team, you will have the same rights a player. However, you will be able to add your roster players, to make them available to the public.

In order to let people get the idea and spirit of your team, fill in the fields in your profile, provide your team logo and your most recent XI. Don;t forget that you are given the ability to look for new players, through the search bar, after logging in.
